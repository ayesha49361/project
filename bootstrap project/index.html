Art & Drawing

Art helps us see connections and brings a more coherent meaning to the world' -Ernest Boyer Visual art has a power beyond words and reaches deep into the core of human consciousness, connecting with us on an emotional and primary level. Observation is the key to be creative and imaginative. This course will offer you an opportunity to observe life around you and acquire better visual aesthetics. During these sessions, we will learn basic and advance levels of visual arts that include drawing and painting from a historical and contemporary perspective. Moreover, it will allow you to interpret your own point of view visually. It is mainly studio-based but students will also have the opportunity to practice in the outside environment.

Course Detail

This course will help students to develop their observation as well as their confidence to create whatever they can imagine. It can also be fruitful to those who want to create paintings, drawings, posters, mood boards, creative presentations, animation and storyboards.
